Built for Students, Founders, and Admins

The TSS platform has been engineered to secure credentials, build ATS-grade resumes, and fast-track HR review loops.

Vetted Onboarding & Opportunities

Candidates can sign up in less than 2 minutes using our mobile-friendly stepper. Real-time validation keeps candidate profiles accurate and opens access to partner openings.

  • Mock OTP Verification: Native email and mobile verification safeguards community entry.
  • Job Posting & Matcher: View active developer, designer, and startup openings posted directly by partners and match dynamically.
  • Status Tracking: Real-time lookup queue monitors application progression.

Admin Control & HR Routing

Administrators manage the vetting queue, review resume credentials, download spreadsheet reports, and log recruiter interactions.

  • Auto-Assign to HRs: Vetted applicant profiles are automatically assigned to recruiters as soon as matching jobs open up.
  • Metrics Board: Overview of processing numbers, verification rates, and weekly registrations.
  • CSV Export: Instant generation of clean applicant tables compatible with Excel.

ATS-Friendly Resume Studio

TSS members get free access to a customized, print-optimized resume builder that implements verified standards for industry resumes.

  • Profile Sync: Sync your verified TSS credentials and education history with the resume generator instantly.
  • Single-Column Layout: Designed to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) cleanly.
  • Fast Exporting: Download standard print-ready PDF files in less than 5 minutes.
